Taste of Nusantara: Explore Indonesia's Culinary Delights

Buras
Buras is made from simple herbs like Bay leaves, and Lemongrass
Coto Makassar
Coto Makassar is made using these primary herbs Galangal, Lemongrass, Bay leaves, Kaffir lime leaves, Coriander seeds, and Tumeric
Es Pisang Ijo
Es Pisang Ijo is a desert made from bananas wrapped in a green rice flour batter, served with coconut milk and syrup
Gogoso
Gogoso is made using these primary herbs Lemongrass, Bay leaves, and Tumeric
Jalangkote
Jalangkote is made using these primary herbs Garlic, Shallots, and Tumeric
Kapurung
Kapurung is made using these primary herbs Lemongrass, Bay leaves, and Tumeric
Mie Titi
Mie Titi is made from simple herbs which are Garlic and Shallots
Nasu Palleko
Nasu Palleko is made using these primary herbs Tumeric, Lemongrass, Galangal, and Bay leaves
Pisang Epe
Pisang Epe is a desert made form grilled bananas topped with palm sugar sauce
Sop Konro
Sop Konro is made using these primary herbs Galangal, Coriander seeds, Nutmeg, bay leaves, Cloves, and Cinnamon
