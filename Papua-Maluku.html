Taste of Nusantara: Explore Indonesia's Culinary Delights

Ikan Bakar Manokwari
Ikan Bakar Manokwari is made using these primary herbs Tumeric, Lemongrass, and Kaffir lime leaves
Ikan Bungkus
Ikan Bungkus is made using these primary herbs Lemongrass, Tumeric, Bay leaves, and Kaffir lime leaves
Ikan Kuah Pala Banda
Ikan Kuah Pala Banda is made using these primary herbs Nutmeg, Tumeric, Lemongrass, Bay leaves, Kaffir lime leaves
Kue Lontar
Kue Lontar is a sweet custard pie made from Eggs, Sugar, and Milk
Kue Vor - Vor
Kue Vor - Vor is a traditional sweet pastry which are made with ingredients like Flour, Sugar, and sometimes Spices
Nasi Kelapa Hitu
Nasi Kelapa Hitu is made using these primary herbs Tumeric, Lemongrass, and Bay leaves
Papeda
Papeda is a traditional sago porridge typically seved with fish soup that may contain herbs like Lemongras, Tumeric, and Kaffir lime leaves
Sambal Colo
Sambal Colo is a fresh and tangy Indonesian chili sauce which is made from a blend of Bird's eye chili, Shallots, Tomatoes, Lemongrass, Kaffir lime leaves, Basil leaves, and Lime juice
Sinole
Sinole is a traditional sago snack made with Coconut and Sugar
Udang Selingkuh
Udang Selingkuh is made from simole herbs such as Lemongrass, and Kaffir lime leaves
