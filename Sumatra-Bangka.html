Taste of Nusantara: Explore Indonesia's Culinary Delights

Ayam Pop
Ayam Pop is made using these primary herbs Galangal, Lemongrass, and Bay leaves
Bika Ambon
Bika Ambon is a cake made with tapioca flour, eggs, sugar, and coconut milk, often flavored with pandan or banana leaves
Dendeng Balado
Dendeng Balado is made using these primary herbs Kaffir lime leaves, Bay leaves, Tumeric, and Galangal
Dodol Agar
Dodol Agar is a sweet made from agar - agar, sugar, and coconut milk, often flavored with pandan
Nasi Gemok
Nasi Gemok is made using these primary herbs Pandan leaves, Lemongrass, and Bay leaves
Pempek
Pempek is a fish cake made from fish and tapioca, typically served with a tangy and spicy vinegar sauce (Cuko)
Rendang
Rendang is made using these primary herbs Tumeric, Galangal, Lemongrass, Kaffir lime leaves, Cinnamon, Cloves, Bay leaves, and Nutmeg
Sate Padang
Sate Padang is made using these primary herbs Tumeric, Coriander seeds, Galangal, Lemongrass, and Kaffir lime leaves
Soto Belitung
Soto Belitung is made using these primary herbs Galangal, Lemongrass, Bay leaves, and Kaffir lime leaves
Tango - Tango
Tango - Tango is made using these primary herbs Tumeric, Lemongrass, and Bay leaves
